How Bitcoin Works and Who Owns It

Covered by: AAPKA KHABRI News Team

Bitcoin is a type of digital currency that operates without any central authority or bank. Transactions are recorded in a shared public ledger known as the blockchain. Bitcoin uses cryptography to secure transactions and control the creation of new units.
